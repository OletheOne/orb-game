Orb Collector Game

Collect orbs by moving your mouse near them. Don't touch the screen edges or you'll lose all your orbs!

Medium: Balanced capture distance and release threshold. (2x points)

  • Collect as many orbs as possible within 60 seconds
  • Each orb is worth 1-5 points (multiplied by difficulty)
  • If you hit the edges, orbs scatter chaotically and you lose points
  • Higher difficulty means harder to capture but more points!
